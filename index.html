Welcome to Ate Bugs!

Enjoy new high modern and ecological nutrition

Ate Bugs is the first Insect Tapas restaurant in Cambodia ever. Our purpose is to show you that insects are delicious! Experience a gastronomical discovery of this typical Cambodian product, through the savoring of sophisticated and appealing tapas, cooked in a fusion of French and Khmer cuisine. Crickets, scorpions, grasshoppers, ants, bees, silkworms, spiders and more, combined with quality Khmer and western products and seasonings, will bring you a delicious and unparalleled taste of adventure! If you are not quite ready yet, please note that most of our dishes can be served without insects.
